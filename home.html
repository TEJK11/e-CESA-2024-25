About Us

Electronics and Computer Engineering Students’ Association (E-CESA) established in 1995, is a crucible where innovation meets mentorship and serves as a vibrant platform for students to engage with the latest technological advancements and industry practices. Our mission is to ignite passion and curiosity in electronics and computer engineering through a diverse range of activities. From organizing workshops on cutting-edge technologies like Raspberry Pi to hosting technical talks with industry professionals, our aim is to be a catalyst for transformation, guiding minds and shaping futures. Beyond our commitment to technical excellence, we are deeply invested in social responsibility, participating in initiatives that make a meaningful impact on our community. Our annual technical magazine, Periscope, is a testament to our dedication, offering insights into the latest technological advancements, interviews with industry leaders, and thought-provoking articles. In the story of our existence, we aim to be the halos that can pinpoint the million little threads causing a giant knot and guide all to understand how to pull them out.

Why we exist

At e-CESA, we believe in the power of hands-on learning and collaboration. Our events, which include project competitions, technical seminars, and interactive sessions, provide valuable opportunities for students to apply their skills and explore potential career paths. In addition to technical activities, we are committed to social responsibility and actively participate in initiatives that benefit our community. Our annual technical magazine, Periscope, showcases the latest in technology, interviews with industry experts, and insightful articles, reflecting our commitment to keeping our members informed and engaged with the tech world. Being a part of e-CESA means more than just participating in events; it’s about gaining essential skills, broadening your knowledge base, and developing leadership and team management capabilities. Join us to be a part of a thriving community that values innovation, learning, and making a positive impact.
